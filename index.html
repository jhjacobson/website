About Me

Hello! My name is Josh Jacobson, and I currently live in Madison, Wisconsin. I grew up in Winona, Minnesota and received my bachelors in Mathematics and Gender Studies from Saint Olaf College in 2014.

I started at Epic immediately after graduation, where I spent 6 years in various leadership and people management positions. I led a portion of our managed services division, where I helped streamline several disparate data entry and reporting systems, ultimately saving our management team hundreds of hours per quarter.

I left Epic in August 2020 to work as a field organizer for the Human Rights Campaign. After the election, my partner and I plan to move to Washington, D.C. where I hope to contribute to the betterment of our country by working in government.
